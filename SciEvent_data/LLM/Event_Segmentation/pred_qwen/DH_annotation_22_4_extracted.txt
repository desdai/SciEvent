[Background]: Caravans of Gold is a multilingual digital exhibit that was built as both a static website and Progressive Web App (PWA).

[Method]: This case study describes the process of developing Caravans of Gold, situating PWAs as minimal computing technologies.

[Results]: Caravans of Gold was built using Wax, a Jekyll-based framework, and demonstrates the similarities between PWA requirements and minimal computing approaches.
