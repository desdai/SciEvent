[Background]: Self-regulation of learning in programming has been extensively investigated, but little attention has been given to social modes of regulation.

[Method]: We developed Thyone, a collaborative Jupyter Notebook extension to enhance co-regulation and motivate learners in an online classroom setting.

[Results]: An exploratory study showed Thyone improved learners' motivation through increased interest, autonomy, and relatedness.
