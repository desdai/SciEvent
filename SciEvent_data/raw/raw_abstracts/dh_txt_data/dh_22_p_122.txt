Caravans of Goldis a multilingual digital exhibit that was built as both a static website and Progressive Web App (PWA). This case study describes the process of developingCaravans of Gold, situating PWAs as minimal computing technologies. PWAs are websites with mobile app features that specifically help disseminate digital content to people who rely on mobile devices for access to the internet.Caravans of Goldwas built using Wax, a Jekyll-based framework, and demonstrates the similarities between PWA requirements and minimal computing approaches.
